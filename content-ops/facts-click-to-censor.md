App page: `/click-to-censor`

## What it is
- A lightweight **Chrome browser extension**.
- Lets you hide sensitive elements on any webpage, in real time.
- Built for developers and creators who share screens or record tutorials.

## How it works
- **Hover** over any element to highlight it, **click** to censor it.
- Works on any element: text, images, tables, and more.
- **Censors persist across page reloads**, saved per-site by CSS selector.
- **Runs 100% locally.** No data leaves the browser. No account needed to use it.

## Modes / features
- **Blur** an element.
- **Blackout** an element.
- **Hide** an element entirely.
- **Click-to-reveal:** temporarily un-censor an element.

## Pricing
- **Blur is free.**
- **Blackout, Hide, and Click-to-reveal are premium.**
- Premium is a **one-time $9.99 lifetime** purchase (not a subscription).
